Rose

The rose is one of the most beautiful and popular flowers in the world. It is known for its attractive petals, pleasant fragrance, and variety of colors. Roses symbolize love, beauty, and friendship. They are widely used in gardens, bouquets, perfumes, and decorations. Roses grow well in sunny environments and require proper care and watering.




Apple

Apple is a nutritious and widely consumed fruit. It is rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants that help maintain good health. Apples come in different colors such as red, green, and yellow. They are eaten fresh and are also used to make juices, pies, jams, and desserts. The saying "An apple a day keeps the doctor away" highlights its health benefits.




Peacock

The peacock is a colorful and majestic bird known for its beautiful feathers. It is the national bird of India and is admired for its graceful appearance. Male peacocks spread their feathers into a magnificent fan during courtship. They are commonly found in forests, gardens, and rural areas. Peacocks are a symbol of beauty and pride.




Bicycle

A bicycle is a simple and eco-friendly mode of transportation. It has two wheels, pedals, handlebars, and a seat. Cycling is a healthy activity that helps improve fitness and reduces pollution. Bicycles are commonly used for travel, exercise, and recreation. They are affordable and suitable for people of all ages.




Banana

Banana is a sweet and healthy fruit enjoyed by people around the world. It is rich in potassium, vitamins, and natural sugars that provide instant energy. Bananas are easy to digest and are commonly eaten as a snack. They are also used in smoothies, desserts, and fruit salads. Banana plants grow well in warm and tropical climates.
